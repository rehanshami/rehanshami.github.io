The project

Spark IT hardware and accessories is an online shop portal that I created. The project involved editing images, researching device descriptions, optimizing the page with SEO, and writing device descriptions and specifications.

Duration: 1 month

Methods: Scoping content, wireframes, image editing, researching, SEO, content writing, and creating customer journey.

Tools: Adobe AEM, Adobe Photoshop, and Figma.

Challenges, insights, and outcomes

This was the first time I worked on an online shop page that directly communicates with an e-commerce platform. This challenge was further increased as this project was to be completed in a short timeframe. I worked closely with the Devices team and sought help from fellow Content Designers to complete this task.

The copy and imagery provided to me required heavy modifications. I edited the imagery in Photoshop and rewrote the copy by referring to device manufacturer websites.

The online shop is live and customers can easily purchase their favorite IT devices.
